Surveying general

On construction sites, surveyors face many different hazards while completing their work. It is important to evaluate the work environment and eliminate as many hazards as possible prior to beginning surveying activities onsite.

A first aid box with enough equipment to cope with the number of workers on site.

An appointed person to take charge of first-aid arrangements.

Information telling workers the name of the appointed person or first aider and where to find them.

An appointed person is someone who is in charge of your first aid arrangements. This includes looking after the equipment, facilities and calling the emergency services. You can have more than one appointed person and they don't need to have any formal training.
